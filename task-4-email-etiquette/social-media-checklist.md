📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist


📌 Introduction

In today’s time, social media has become an important part of a student’s daily routine. It is widely used for communication, sharing ideas, gaining knowledge, and entertainment. However, careless use can lead to issues such as privacy risks, online harassment, and harm to one’s reputation.

Therefore, it is essential for students to use social media wisely and responsibly. The following are some important do’s and don’ts that every college student should keep in mind.


✅ Do’s for Students

1. Maintain Your Privacy
Regularly review your privacy settings
Share personal information only with trusted people
2. Verify Before You Share
Always confirm the accuracy of information
Avoid forwarding unverified or false content
3. Use Social Media Positively
Highlight your skills, achievements, and projects
Create a strong and positive online identity
4. Think Before Posting
Understand that online posts can remain permanent
Review your content carefully before sharing
5. Show Respect Online
Communicate politely
Be respectful towards others’ opinions and feelings


❌ Don’ts for Students

1. Stay Away from Cyberbullying
Do not post offensive or hurtful comments
Avoid participating in any form of online harassment
2. Don’t Share Sensitive Information
Never disclose personal details like phone numbers, passwords, or bank information
3. Avoid Plagiarism
Do not copy others’ work without permission
Always give credit where it is due
4. Don’t Post Negative or Harmful Content
Avoid posting anything disrespectful about your college, teachers, or peers
Maintain a professional and positive image
5. Be Careful with Links
Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
Protect yourself from scams and malware


🎯 Conclusion

Social media can be very beneficial if used thoughtfully. By following these simple guidelines, students can ensure their safety, maintain a good reputation, and make the most out of digital platforms.

👉 Responsible usage today can shape your future positively.
